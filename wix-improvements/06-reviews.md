# 6. Reviews near Book Now

Your public site currently shows Housecall Pro booking/portal CTAs, but no clear review widget. Add social proof next to booking.

## Option A — Housecall Pro reviews (preferred if you already use HCP reviews)

1. Log into Housecall Pro.  
2. Find **Marketing / Online Booking / Reviews** (wording varies by HCP version).  
3. Copy your public review link or embed/widget code.  
4. In Wix Editor on **Home** and **Services**:
   - Add a section titled **What customers say**
   - Place it directly under or beside **Book Now**
   - Paste the HCP widget (Custom Embed / HTML iframe) **or** add 3–5 short testimonials + a button: **Read our reviews**

## Option B — Google reviews (strong for local SEO)

1. Open your Google Business Profile.  
2. Copy the public reviews / Place URL.  
3. In Wix:
   - **Add → Apps** → search “Google Reviews” (or use a simple text + button section)
   - Or add an HTML embed from a reviews app you already pay for
4. Place on Home (below hero or next to Book Now) and on Services.

### Fallback if no widget

Add this section manually:

**Heading:** Trusted by homeowners across Benbrook & Fort Worth  

**Body:**  
See why local homeowners book Willingham Irrigation for sprinkler repair and outdoor services.

**Button:** Read Google Reviews → *(your GBP reviews URL)*  
**Secondary button:** Book Now → HCP booking URL  

If you have 3 written 5-star quotes, paste them as cards (name + city only — no private info).
