Make your home or business stand out this season with a custom Christmas light installation from Willingham Irrigation. We design a clean, professional look that follows your roofline, garage, and windows — then we install it, take it down after the holidays, and leave the lights with you.

### What’s included
- Custom layout designed for your property
- Professional install of C9-style lighting
- Lights you own (purchase, not lease)

### What’s not included (set expectations)
- Interior holiday decorating

### How the process works
1. **Request a quote** — share your address and photos if you have them.  
2. **Site review** — we confirm roofline, outlets, and scope.  
3. **Install** — clean, secure placement with a finished look.  
4. **Enjoy the season** — lights stay up through the holidays.  
5. **Takedown** — we remove the display and leave your lights with you.

### Timing
Holiday lighting is seasonal. Book early for the best install windows before Thanksgiving and early December. Takedown is scheduled after the holidays (typically early January).

### Pricing
Every property is different, so we quote based on roofline length, complexity, and fixture count. You’ll get a clear written quote before any work begins. Ask about discounts available with our irrigation maintenance plans.
