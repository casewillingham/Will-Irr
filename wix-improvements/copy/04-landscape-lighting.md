Landscape Lighting That Looks Great and Works Hard

### Intro
Professional landscape lighting makes your property safer, more usable after dark, and more attractive from the curb. Willingham Irrigation designs and installs outdoor lighting systems that highlight what you love about your yard — without glare, clutter, or guesswork.

Whether you want soft accent lighting for trees and beds, clear path lighting for walkways, architectural lighting for your home’s best features, or brighter coverage for security, we build a system that fits your property and goals.

### Who it’s for
Homeowners and property managers in Benbrook, Fort Worth, Cleburne, Aledo, White Settlement, Palmilla Springs, and surrounding areas who want:

- Better curb appeal at night
- Safer walkways and entries
- More usable outdoor living space after sunset
- Reliable, energy-efficient fixtures that last

### Lighting options we offer

**Landscape & accent lighting**  
Highlight trees, planting beds, and yard focal points with natural-looking, carefully aimed lights.

**Path & walkway lighting**  
Improve visibility along sidewalks, steps, and driveways with low, subtle fixtures that blend into the landscape.

**Architectural lighting**  
Bring out your home’s structure — columns, facades, entryways — with clean placement that adds depth without looking overdone.

**Security & area lighting**  
Increase visibility around side yards, corners, and dark zones with dependable coverage that feels inviting, not harsh.

### Benefits
- Stronger curb appeal after dark  
- Safer paths for family, guests, and deliveries  
- Extended evening use of patios and yards  
- Energy-efficient fixtures with a polished, professional look  
- Designed around your landscape — not a one-size template  

### How the process works
1. **Quote / site visit** — we walk the property and discuss goals.  
2. **Simple design plan** — fixture types, locations, and zones.  
3. **Install** — clean wiring and placement with attention to finish.  
4. **Walkthrough** — we aim lights with you so the night look is right.  

### What’s typically included
- Design guidance for fixture placement
- Professional installation
- Energy-efficient outdoor-rated fixtures (as quoted)
- Aiming / focusing at install
- Clear quote before work begins

### FAQ

**Is this the same as holiday lighting?**  
No. Landscape lighting is a year-round outdoor lighting system. Holiday lighting is a seasonal Christmas display (see our Holiday Lighting page).

**Do you only do irrigation?**  
Irrigation repair and maintenance is our core business. Landscape and holiday lighting are additional outdoor services we offer in the same service area.

**Can lighting be added in phases?**  
Often yes — start with paths and entries, then add accent zones later. Ask when you request a quote.
